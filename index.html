Breath First Search

About

Follow along and catch your breath or set the animation to match your lung capacity.


  1. Prepare for a deep breath, completely exhale
  2. Press and hold the shining INHALE button while inhaling./li>
  3. Let go of the button when you are ready to exhale
  4. Follow the visual guide and discover your initial mindful breath.


About the project:

I explored guided meditation audio to improve my mental health. I appreciated the positive reinforcement, but struggled with keeping my breathing in line with the narrator. To manage this, I created Breath First Search, my algorithm for mindful breathing.
